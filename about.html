WHO WE ARE?

With its huge, highly equipped manufacturing units based in Faridabad (Haryana) JAV forgings is capable of serving forging needs of all the major industries like petroleum, defense, heavy engineering, paper, sugar, nuclear/electrical power plants etc. With ultimate vision and commitment of its leaders of Chandhok family, JAV Forgings is bound to lead the forging manufacturing industry for decades to come.
